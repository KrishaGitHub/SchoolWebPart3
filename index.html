Campus Life

School Church

Was established in 1978

The church was dedicated to Saint Francis Xavier; but, from the first, it was familiarly known as the College Church. It was the first English-speaking parish in the city, and served primarily the growing Irish population of St. Louis.



School Library

Was build in 1980

A beautifully designed, well-lit, well-furnished and spacious library is located centrally giving easy access to all the sections of the school. Its collection of over 30,000 books is rapidly being enhanced. Students are encouraged to borrow books and read in their free time so that reading becomes a habit.



School auditorium

Was build in 1982

The stage has always been held in great honour at St. Xaviers. For an educational institution an auditorium is not just a venue for school or college events, or merely a meeting place, it is a space where a corporation of students and scholars congregate to celebrate, to debate, to discuss, and sometimes even to mourn collectively.
